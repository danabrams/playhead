1. Qwen3-0.6B (bundled specialist model, fine-tuned + 4-bit quantized)
------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Playhead bundles an on-device ad-detection model that is a DERIVATIVE of
Qwen3-0.6B: fine-tuned on Playhead's own data and 4-bit dynamically quantized,
then compiled to Apple's Core AI (.aimodel) format.

  Built with Qwen.
  Qwen3 is developed by Alibaba Cloud / the Qwen team.
  Licensed under the Apache License, Version 2.0.

  This product includes a modified version of Qwen3-0.6B. Modifications:
  supervised fine-tuning on Playhead ad-detection data and 4-bit dynamic
  weight quantization. The bundled weights are a derivative work.

  Upstream: https://huggingface.co/Qwen/Qwen3-0.6B
  License:  Apache-2.0 (see https://www.apache.org/licenses/LICENSE-2.0)
